Random Crystal Madness!

A random amount of crystals will appear at the start of the game.

Click the four crystals below to add a specific amount to your score. Each crystal is worth a specific amount of gems. Be careful the amount is hidden!

If you can get your score to match the random amount of gems, you win! But, if you go over, you lose...

After each win or loss the goal amount and the amount each crystal adds will change!
